How to use this form

  • Go to the kit page in AddGene, e.g. https://www.addgene.org/kits/moclo-ytk for the MoClo kit
  • Go to the tab "Contents of Kit"
  • In the "Inventory" section, display all items of the list by clicking on the dropdown that initially says "Show 10 entries" and select "All"
  • In the browser, right-click an empty part of the page, and click on "Save as..." (in Chrome) or "Save page as..." (in Firefox)
  • Select format: "Webpage, HTML Only"
  • Save the file to your computer
